Why We’re Fundraising

I moved from the US to Naples, Italy about 2 years ago and the biggest question in my head at the time was why am I here in Naples? There was some chaos, driving around I learned there were alot of hand signals, especially in traffic My family and I didn’t know anyone, and I had a completely new job that I had no experience in. None of this made sense, none of this was according to my plan. My family and I kept coming to Celebration Caserta and week in and week out people from church surrounded us with love here in church and out on in the workplace. Acquaintances became friends and mentors in our daily lives. Then it hit me I’m here to be an active part of the Church. Just as others are pouring into me Sunday and throughout the week. This totally changed my perspective It’s not about what job, or title I held, or how being stationed here at Naples was going to impact my career. I am here to serve others at Church and be a light for Him in the workplace. He shifted my selfish perspective to a perspective that focused on that which is eternal. All because of an open seat surrounded by the active body of Christ. Thats just one of the many truths I’ve learned by being a part of Celebration Caserta. I want others to learn and be surrounded by the church as I have here. But we are running out of seats! So what can you do now? Join me in the Building Hope Relay where we will do a run and walk relay with each other for 24 hours. Anyone can pledge various amounts per mile or pledge a flat rate, and we will use these funds to build a new home for Celebration Caserta. From 15 May to 16 May at Carney Park we will run, walk, eat, pray and spend some focused family time together with the purpose to create more room for more chairs so when those who need a seat as we all do, don’t get left to the wayside because there wasn’t room. You help will impact lives for years to come.

How The Relay Works

Relay team members run, walk, jog, push strollers for 24 continuous hours

Participants rotate in and out. Everyone moves at there own pace

During the relay you will be able to watch the mile count add up live, and at the end of the relay the miles will be verified and uploaded to the fund site. The fund site will then automatically add up funds, and they will all be put towards a building for Celebration Caserta.

How You Can Help

You can support this mission in a few simple but meaningful ways. First, consider giving—whether it’s a one-time donation or a pledge per mile, every contribution helps us create more space for people to hear the Word and grow in community.

You can also participate by joining us at Carney Park from 15–16 May for the 24-hour Building Hope Relay. Come run, walk, spend time with others, and be part of what God is doing.

Finally, you can pray and share. Pray for the lives that will be impacted through this new space, and share this page with others so more people can be part of building something that will last for generations.
